About the creator

Mary, cloud specialist

Hey there! I'm Mary and I with my little studio live in Riga, Latvia.

I have tried and experimented quite a lot to create a mobile, that would be 100% unique. Thus I invented my first clouds and since then my house has become a real cloud workspace.

For 3 years I have worked as an inventor, designer, photographer, director, manager, copywriter and even a postal courier at Fetroll studio. It's me who answers all your questions, who collects your mobiles and sends parcels all over the world.

I am very proud to make adorable decor for your nursery and to help you create one and only interior for your sweet little ones. Thank you so much for choosig Fetroll studio!
